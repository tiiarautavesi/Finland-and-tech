Why Finland needs technological innovations?

Real news

In the world we live in information can be accessed in so many platforms, that even for an individual it’s hard to keep track on all of them.

It’s very hard to know what’s true online and what’s not. A person who feels threatened by the society might not want to know the truth and only seek for information that they want to hear. This opens a door for manipulation and can be used against the society for example in political context.

To solve the problem we need to be able to enhance the consumers rights for real news and improve the media reading skills with developing the algorithms to support the greater good. People need to be aware of the real threats our country, Finland, and our planet are experiencing and have sources for how to help an take part in positive change.

Climate change

Finland’s nature is one of its most precious treasures. We need to protect the fauna and flora to the best of our abilities. To reduce the pollution we desperately need technological innovations that help us cut out unsustainable energy sources.

Education, work & technology

It’s widely known fact that robots and technological innovations are taking over professions that many of us have had or currently have. It’s impossible to predict the timeframe of this economical and social change.

We as humans seek purpose and meaning through our careers and work. What will replace this? Maybe we will all become artists.

When jobs such as police, attorney, doctor and teacher are overtaken with robots, we are offering unreasonable responsibility and authority to the tech companies providing these services. Humankind as customers have to be on very high educational level to keep on track with everything, so we can understand what’s happening and take action when needed.

We need to adapt deep level technological studies to the education system so we can stay on top of the wave instead of being drowned by it.
